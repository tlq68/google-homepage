How can I create my own webpage?

One way is to register a domain name. You are probably going to have to pay to keep it maintained if you go this route. After you get a domain name, you need to find a way to host your website . There are many companies that offer this serice. You will inevitably need content, so, be sure to have that. And then just integrate your content with the domain name and have it hosted. Google will tell you that it is hard to build an effective website... But honestly, a basic website is pretty easy to make after learning some of the most basic HTML and CSS.
